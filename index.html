About SuperAxeCoin

Mining For Fun, Not Profit

SuperAxeCoin is a hobbyist cryptocurrency built for people who want to learn about blockchain technology, experiment with mining, and have fun doing it. We're not here to promise you riches or "moon" - we're here to provide a real, working blockchain that you can mine, tinker with, and learn from.

Based on modern Bitcoin Core, SuperAxeCoin includes all the latest features like Taproot, SegWit, and Schnorr signatures active from genesis. With 2-minute blocks and LWMA difficulty adjustment, it's designed to be accessible and responsive for small-scale miners and learners alike.
